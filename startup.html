PROBLEM
SCATTERED MEMORIES

Most memories are created by a group of people. Family gatherings, weddings, parties, concerts and sport events, these are the common scenarios in which people get bonded with each other. The need to link and share the collective memories is simple and real, but the existing solution is far from ideal. After the event, all the precious memories were scattered among everyone’s phones. All the thoughts, feelings and emotions are fragmented on everyone’s social media. It was really tiring to gather all of them.

IDEATION
MOMENT?

“You know how everyone’s always saying seize the moment? I don’t know, I’m kind of thinking it’s the other way around. You know, like the moment seizes us. “

Space and time are universal forms of the existence of matter. We cannot recover a single moment of existence, but we can try to recall the memory of living in that specific moment.

What do people usaully do to keep our memories? They hang the precious photos and on the wall. On DIITU, you keep your memories in an intuitive way, posting on a wall, just like you always do. With your cellphone, you can access your beloved memories anytime and anywhere.

PROCESS
WIREFRAME & FLOW

Wireframe and flowchart are good tools to try out new ideas and make sure all the team members reach consensus. We all make mistakes, but fail fast could save our time.

SOLUTION
SEIZE THE MOMENTS

With our algorithm using the factors such as time and location, DIITU automatically aggregate the collective memories. All the related moments are linked together. Now you won't miss a thing.

Contents come first. Users would definitely not going to like it if the interface stole their spotlights. For that reason, the interface should stay simple, honoring the content and keep the layout elegant. It has to appropriately display the content with style.

Wall is a vessel for your precious memories. With your phone, you cas access your beloved memories anytime and anywhere.

THE JOURNEY IN A START-UP

After being admitted to Institute of Applied Art in NCTU, I got a few months before the new semester begins. That was when I joined a startup team called DIITU. Since then I was the only designer in the team until 2015.

Working in startup team was no doubt the most unforgettable experience in my life. Even there were only five members in our team and the resources were so limited, we made the most of it and created our greatest value. The passion and thrill that comes with challenges are very addicted. Moreover, I was free to express my opinion and all of my works made a real difference in business.

The experience of joining a startup team really makes me stand out among my classmates. It provides me a broader horizon and makes me see things with different perspective.
